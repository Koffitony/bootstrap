WorldSkills São Paulo 2015

Brazil: a multicultural country. The Brazilian population is composed of a great diversity of races and creeds, whose legacy is due to immigrants from different regions of our planet: cheerful and welcoming people that have worked extensively in the construction of an economically competitive and socially fair nation.

Our leaders and the whole society have been building a full awareness of the importance of Education to consolidate these goals.

The Vocational Education Event - the Global Meeting Point

For the first time held in Latin America and for the second time in southern hemisphere, the biggest Vocational Education event will feature more than 60 countries/regions, involving more than 1,000 Competitors, over 1,000 Experts, as well as educational, government and industry leaders. It will surely be the largest vocational event, to be visited by over 200,000 people, including about 100,000 youngsters from Elementary Schools.

Vocational Education’s moment

It is a special moment for Brazil and other countries in the Americas, when mankind – through its rulers and global institutions structured to build social equity based on knowledge and education – will participate in an event of such magnitude and this experience will certainly attest to everyone the importance of vocational education as an essential and effective strategy for the social and economic transformation of communities and countries.
